Help us

May be you doesn't know it for now, but Skimbo is an open-source project. You can join our team to help us to improve Skimbo as you like it.

Translation

You can find Skimbo's translation files on Github. To help us to translate Skimbo, you just have to click on the "Edit" button (on the top right of the document).
